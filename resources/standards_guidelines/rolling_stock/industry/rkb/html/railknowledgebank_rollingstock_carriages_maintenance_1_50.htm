Title A collaborative approach to bringing railcar manufacturing to Western Australia
 
Author Taylor, G
Affiliation Public Transport Authority of Western Australia
Source Railway Technical Society of Australasia (RTSA)
Conference CORE 2021: Collaborating to Master Complexity: Conference on Railway Excellence, 21-23 June 2021, Perth, WA, Australia
Publication Date 2021
Abstract This paper provides an overview of the METRONET railcar procurement project which is delivering new electric and diesel railcars plus a new train maintenance and stabling depot at Bellevue, to the east of Perth. It identifies some of the key requirements and complexities of the project, some of the key stakeholders and the role that collaboration has played in addressing the requirements. It concludes that the project is well positioned to deliver the requirements for the State and that Alliance or Partnership contracts are not essential to developing a collaborative approach to project delivery.
